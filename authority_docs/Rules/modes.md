# Game Modes & Advanced Timing

## Two Player Mode
- Standard 40 HP each
- Full market availability
- First player draws 3 cards, second player draws 4

### Victory Conditions
- Reduce opponent to 0 HP
- Win by third market depletion with higher HP
- Win if opponent cannot draw cards

## Multiplayer (3-4 Players)

### Free-for-all
- Each player acts independently
- Can attack any opponent
- Last monarch standing wins

### Team Battle (4 Players)
- Two teams of two players
- Shared market phase
- Individual resources and HP

## Special Rules

- Turn Order: clockwise from first player
- Market refresh happens after each turn
- Political rules: no showing cards, no giving/trading cards

## Advanced Rules & Timing

### Priority Order
- Start of turn triggers
- Defending unit abilities
- Attacking unit abilities
- Market/recruitment effects
- End of turn effects

### Simultaneous Effects
- Active player resolves first, then choose ordering for other players' triggers
