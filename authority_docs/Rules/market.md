# Market System

## Market Structure

- 6 face-up cards available
- General cards set aside separately
- Market deck contains remaining faction cards
- Empty slots filled at end of turn

## Market Depletion

- When deck empties, count as one depletion
- Shuffle discarded market cards to form new deck
- Game ends after third depletion
- Cannot deplete market during recruitment

## Market Actions

### Recruitment
- Use unit power as authority (1:1 ratio)
- Multiple units can contribute authority
- Must pay exact cost or overpay
- Recruited units go to discard pile

### Market Refresh
- Fill empty slots at end of turn
- If no purchases, next player may swap one card
- Swapped card goes to bottom of market deck
- Must maintain 6 cards when possible

### General Recruitment
- Available throughout game
- Count as faction cards
- Limited to one copy per player
- Enter general zone when recruited
