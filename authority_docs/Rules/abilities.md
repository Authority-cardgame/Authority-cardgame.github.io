# Card Abilities

## Ability Types

### Passive Abilities
- Active while unit is in specified zone
- No activation cost
- Apply continuously
- Stack with other passive abilities

### Triggered Abilities
- "When attacking": triggers on attack declaration
- "When defending": active while in defense row
- "When recruited": triggers when bought from market

### Active Abilities
- Require discarding unit to activate
- Cannot be used while defending
- Resolve immediately when used

## Ability Keywords

### Combat Keywords
- Ignore: Unit doesn't block but keeps abilities
- Disable: Unit doesn't block or use abilities
- Pierce: Damage continues to next row
- Protect: Prevents damage to target

### Zone Keywords
- Retire: Remove from game voluntarily
- Annihilate: Force remove from game
- Discard: Move to discard pile
- General: Move to general zone

### Value Keywords
- "X": Row number unit is in
- "2X": Double the row number
- "+N" / "-N": Adjust by fixed amount
