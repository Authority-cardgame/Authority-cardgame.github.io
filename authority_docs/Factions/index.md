# Factions & Components

This page gathers faction summaries, card breakdowns, and component lists.

## Factions
- **Royal Soldiers**: Balanced and reliable forces
- **Bloodrunners**: Aggressive, fast-paced tactics
- **Iron Wall**: Defensive fortification specialists
- **House of Golden Ox**: Economic and recruitment experts
- **Hooded Brothers**: Evasion and disruption specialists

## Card Breakdown

### Starting Deck (per player)
- 3× Squire (2 power)
- 3× Man-at-arms (3 power)
- 2× Knight (4 power)
- 1× Kingsguard (special defense)

### Faction Cards
- Royal Soldiers (36 cards)
- Bloodrunners (26 cards)
- Iron Wall (26 cards)
- House of Golden Ox (26 cards)
- Hooded Brothers (26 cards)

### General Cards
- Royal Soldiers: 4 unique generals
- Other Factions: 3 unique generals each

## Components

- 4 Starting Player Decks (8 cards each)
- 140 Faction Cards
- 16 General Cards
- 4 Kingsguard Cards
- 4 Life Point Trackers (40 HP each)
- Market Depletion Counter (3 max)
- First Player Token
- Authority Tokens

## Card Anatomy

- **Unit Stats**: Power, Cost, Name, Faction Symbol
- **Card Elements**: Ability text box, art window, type line, rarity indicator
